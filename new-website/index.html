My Time at HYPE

Nonprofit Work and Coding


During 2022, I had the privelege of working with a nonprofit HYPE (Hope for Youth through Education), as a lead instructor. HYPE focueses on providing free education and mentorship for young girls of color to break through the tech industry. As a lead instructor, I was able to teach these young women basic coding and web development skills such as, HTML, CSS, and even robotics.


Lead Instructor

Some responsibilities as a lead instructor included creating lesson plans, teaching basic principles of development, and providing mentorship for the TAs. I was an instructor for two different programs, the summer and fall program. The summer program was in person program with local boys and girls club. The fall program was an online after school program that was open to anyone.



My Experience

Overall it was an incredible experience to work along amazing women who already had so much experience in the field and to inspire young women to pursue STEM. Especially during my time working the girls from the boys and girls club, I learned how much they just needed one person to believe in them. It was such a rewarding experience to see them accomplish finishing a website at the end of the program.
